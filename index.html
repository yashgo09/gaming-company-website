About Tripo

Your Source for Mobile Entertainment

Our specialty is mobile gaming. We develop games of different categories like Casual, RPG, Action, Adventure, and many more. Most of our games are free-to-play and available on all mobile devices and even tablets.

Our Games

Here are some of our top featured and popular games. These are available on both Play Store and App Store.

Kipon

Casual - Free

Kipon is a casual game that provides an experience similar to the classic game Mario. It is not the exact copy of Mario but many concepts are similar to it. People who like old classical games will sure like this one.

Robotrix

RPG - Free

Robotrix is an RPG (Role Playing Game). It is based on modern robotics technology. You will see action, adventure, story, and of course high-tech weapons and equipment. The storyline of this game is based on the future.

Treasure Box

Adventure - Free

Treasure Box is an adventure game based on ancient time treasure stories. The goal of this game is to find treasure by visiting different places and finding clues. You can also compete with your friends by playing online multiplayer mode.
